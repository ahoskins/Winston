Hello and welcome to Winston.

Winston is a beautiful schedule builder for U of A students. It's meant to prevent wrangling with BearTracks trying to find the optimal schedule, and instead make you happy and full of joy.

Look for new features rolling out like preferences, rate-my-prof integration, and sharing.

For the technical folk, this page was build with Angular JS and the site runs on a Python backend. The creators are Ross Anderson and Andrew Hoskins.
